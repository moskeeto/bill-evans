Bill Evans | Pianist | Jazz Pioneer

Biography

Bill Evans (born William John Evans) was born in Plainfield, New Jersey on August 16, 1929. Evans was an American jazz pianist known for his lush harmonies and lyrical improvisation. Evans is one of the most influential pianists of his time.

Bill's first piano teacher was his mother; he also studied violin and flute.

Notable songs

  • Nardis
  • Waltz for Debby
  • Autumn Leaves
  • Peace Piece
  • Darn that Dream
